# ge-script — Script & Hook Writer (Step 2 of 8)

## STEP 0 — INTAKE HARD STOP
Confirm: the **chosen title**, the **primary keyword**, the **format**
(Reaction / FAQ / "What to do if…" / Local-explainer), target **length**
(default 6–10 min long-form), and the **city** for local references. If a source
document (case article, transcript, statute) is provided, read it first.

## STEP 0.5 — LOAD SHARED PROTOCOL
Read `ge-compliance/SKILL.md`. Insert a spoken "not legal advice" line (Disclaimer
A) within the first 60 seconds and again near the CTA. For reaction content add
Disclaimer C; for any result mention add Disclaimer B. Strip all banned language.

## STEP 1 — HOOK (0–10s)
Write the hook with NO greeting, NO logo, NO "hey guys." Lead with stake/payoff.
Offer 2 hook variants using one of:
- **Bold claim** ("Most people lose their case in the first 24 hours — here's why.")
- **Question** ("Arrested and the police didn't read your rights? Here's what that
  actually means.")
- **Result-first / curiosity** ("There's one sentence that can wreck your injury
  claim. I'll tell you what it is.")
Target: >70% retention at 30 seconds.

## STEP 2 — STAY BLOCK (10–30s)
Establish relevance + light credibility ("I've handled **[practice]** cases in
**[CITY]** for [X] years" — never "expert/best"), then promise the structure
("Three things you need to know…"). Insert Disclaimer A here.

## STEP 3 — BODY
- Plain language, short sentences, analogies for legal concepts.
- **Pattern interrupt every 60–90 seconds** (question, example, "but here's the
  catch," B-roll cue, on-screen text cue).
- For local-explainer: cite the **[CITY/STATE]** rule generically and accurately;
  if unsure of the statute, write `[VERIFY STATUTE]` rather than inventing one.
- Mid-roll **re-hook** at the midpoint ("Now the part most people get wrong…").

## STEP 4 — CTA (compliant)
Soft CTA: invite the viewer to watch the related full video / subscribe for more
**[city] legal explainers** / contact the firm for their specific situation. Insert
Disclaimer A again and the attribution. No outcome promises.

## STEP 5 — OUTPUT FORMAT
Deliver:
1. **Script** with timecodes and `[B-ROLL]` / `[ON-SCREEN TEXT]` cues.
2. **Clip-able moments list** (timestamp + why it's a good Short) → feeds ge-shorts.
3. The STEP-0.5 **COMPLIANCE CHECK** block.

## STEP 6 — HANDOFF
> **Next step:** Run **ge-package** for titles/thumbnails and **ge-seo** for the
> description, using this script and the primary keyword. Run **ge-shorts** on the
> clip-able moments list.
