# ge-community — Community Engagement Routine (Step 7 of 8)

## STEP 0 — INTAKE HARD STOP
Confirm: the **published video title/URL**, the **topic**, and any **common
questions** you already expect. If the video is a reaction/commentary piece, note
it (replies must avoid commenting on pending-case specifics).

## STEP 0.5 — LOAD SHARED PROTOCOL
Read `ge-compliance/SKILL.md`. Every public reply must avoid giving
case-specific legal advice (which can risk an inadvertent attorney-client
relationship / UPL issues) and must avoid outcome promises. Use the "general
information only — consult a lawyer about your situation" framing.

## STEP 1 — PINNED COMMENT
Draft one pinned comment that:
- Asks a **discussion question** tied to the video ("Has this happened to you in
  **[CITY]**? What did you do?").
- Adds a **compliant CTA** (watch the full video / subscribe / free consultation
  link) with the disclaimer note.

## STEP 2 — FIRST-HOUR REPLY PLAN
The first ~60 minutes after publish drive early engagement signals. Provide a
short plan: reply to the first ~10 comments, ask follow-up questions to extend
threads, heart genuinely helpful comments, and avoid copy-paste spam.

## STEP 3 — REUSABLE REPLY TEMPLATES (5)
Draft 5 templates for common comment types, each helpful but compliant:
```
1. "Asking for specific legal advice" → empathize + general info + invite a
   consultation + "I can't give advice on your specific situation here."
2. "Sharing their own situation" → acknowledge + general direction + consult-locally note.
3. "Disagreement / argument" → respectful, factual, no escalation, no outcome claims.
4. "Compliment / thanks" → warm + subscribe nudge + next-video tease.
5. "Off-topic / troll" → brief, professional, or ignore; never argue.
```
Each template ends with the condensed disclaimer where advice-adjacent.

## STEP 4 — COMMUNITY IDENTITY (optional)
Suggest a community name/identity convention (the way LegalEagle uses "Legal
Eagles") to build belonging — keep it on-brand for **Great Elephant Law** and
non-misleading.

## STEP 5 — OUTPUT + HANDOFF
Deliver the pinned comment + first-hour plan + 5 templates + identity idea + the
COMPLIANCE CHECK.
> **Next step:** After 48 hours (and at 7/28 days), run **ge-analyze**.
